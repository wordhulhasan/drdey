Srabana Maiti

Srabana Maiti is a Master’s Student in Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at North Dakota State University under the supervision of Dr. Subhashish Dey. She has completed her Bachelor of Technology degree from Heritage Institute of Technology, Kolkata. She has done a summer training on IoT with KernelSphere Technologies. She has also worked as an Electronics Tutor with LearnElectronics where is curated video lecture courses on IoT and C++ Programming. Currently she is working as a Lab Assistant for Digital Design and Energy Conversion. She will be pursuing her Master’s Thesis on Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) based sensors for precision agriculture and smart buildings.
